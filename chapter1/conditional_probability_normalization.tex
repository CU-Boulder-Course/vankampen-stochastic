\leveldown{Normalization of conditional probability - pg. 11}

\leveldown{Problem}

Prove and interpret the normalization of the conditional probability

\begin{equation*}
\int P_{A|B}(a, b) da = 1 \, .
\end{equation*}

\levelstay{Solution}

Use Bayes's rule:
\begin{align*}
\int P_{A|B}(a, b) da
&= \frac{1}{P_B(b)} \int P_{A,B}(a, b) \, da \\
&= \frac{P_B(b)}{P_B(b)} \\
&= 1 \, .
\end{align*}
